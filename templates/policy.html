Last updated: January 15, 2024

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you subscribe to our newsletter, create an account, or contact us. This may include your name, email address, and any other information you choose to provide.

Our newsletter service is powered by Amazon SES (Simple Email Service), ensuring reliable delivery and professional email management. By subscribing to our newsletter, you agree to receive periodic emails containing sports news, updates, and promotional content.

We use Amazon SES to deliver our newsletters and email communications. This service helps us maintain high deliverability rates and professional email management while adhering to industry best practices.

We typically send newsletters 2-3 times per week, containing the latest sports news, analysis, and updates. We may send additional emails for breaking news or special announcements.

Our emails contain:
